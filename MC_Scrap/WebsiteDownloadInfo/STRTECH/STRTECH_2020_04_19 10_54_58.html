COMPANY OVERVIEW

Sterlite Technologies Ltd`s business activities involve manufacturing of telecommunication cables.The company has expanded their portfolio to manufacture and supply Optical Fibers, Fiber Optic Cables, Power Transmission Conductors, Aluminum & Alloy Rods, Copper Telecom Cables, Structured Data Cables, and Customer Premise Equipment and Cable Accessories.Their products cater to various sectors such as telecom, defense, oil and gas, railway, roadways, power, aviation and broadband..The company also engagged in the business of Telecom, Power Product and Solutions.
