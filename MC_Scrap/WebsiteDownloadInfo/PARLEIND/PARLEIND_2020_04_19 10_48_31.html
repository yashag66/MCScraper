COMPANY OVERVIEW

Parle Software Ltd is an India-based supplier of enterprise solutions and services, such as enterprise resource application, knowledge management, supply chain and e-Business applications. The company is engaged in information technology business. The company provides technology services and delivers it to clients operating in various industry segments. The company also engaged in export of software products and software development services to countries like USA, UK and Hong Kong. It is headquartered in Mumbai, India.
